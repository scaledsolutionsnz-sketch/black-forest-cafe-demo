Our Story

A Cosy Corner
in Naseby

Black Forest Cafe is a beloved local spot nestled in the charming village of Naseby, Central Otago. We're known for our friendly service, honest home-cooked food, and coffee that keeps the locals coming back.

Whether you've just come off the Naseby Forest trails, are visiting for the curling rink, or simply passing through — we're here to make your day a little better.

Our Story

Naseby's Favourite Gathering Place

Nestled on Derwent Street in the heart of Naseby, Black Forest Cafe has been the go-to spot for locals and travellers alike since 2006. Named after the ancient forest that wraps around this charming Central Otago town, the cafe carries the same warmth and character that earned it the title of "best little cafe in New Zealand."

In 2022, Kirsty Sloan — a chef who had long loved this place — took the reins and restored the Black Forest name to where it belongs. She crafts a menu that honours what made this cafe great: real ingredients, fresh home baking every day, and coffee pulled with genuine care.

Whether you're warming up by the roaring fireplace after a ride on the Otago Central Rail Trail, or simply stopping in for a flat white and a slice, you'll always leave feeling like a local.

What We Offer

Something For Everyone

Coffee & Drinks

Award-worthy flat whites, long blacks, and cappuccinos — the kind of coffee that earns "best in New Zealand" reviews from guests who travel far and wide.

All-Day Breakfast

Eggs Benedict, Croque Madame, filled rolls, and hearty morning plates — served all day so you can breakfast entirely on your own schedule.

Lunch & Mains

Pulled pork burgers, nachos loaded with toppings, fresh salads, wraps, and hot pies. Well priced, well portioned, and made with genuine local pride.

Cabinet & Baking

Housemade Black Forest slices, melting moments, custard tarts, caramel slice, and seasonal cakes — baked fresh in-house every single day.
